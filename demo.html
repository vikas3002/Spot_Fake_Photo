📸 Spot the Fake Photo — Live Demo

Point your camera at something real, then at a phone/laptop/monitor showing a picture. This runs a simplified client-side version of the moiré / glare / bezel heuristic from predict.py — 100% on-device, nothing leaves your browser.

What it's doing live: downsamples each frame to 64×64 gray, runs a small in-browser FFT to score pixel-grid/moiré peakiness, checks for blown-out specular glare, and looks for a strong rectangular bezel edge inside the frame. These are the same signals used in features.py — this page is a lightweight JS port for a live demo, not a 1:1 copy of the Python model.

Privacy: video never leaves the device — everything here runs in your browser.
